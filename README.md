# Electronics-Engineering-Library

A curated collection of essential books, references, and specifications on Electronic Engineering, Circuit Design, Signal Integrity, and Embedded Systems.

---

## 📚 Book List & Folder Structure

### 📁 [01_Circuit_Design](./01_Circuit_Design/)
Focuses on fundamental and advanced analog/digital circuit design, component behavior, and practical hardware engineering:
* **The Art of Electronics (3rd Edition)** — *Paul Horowitz, Winfield Hill*
* **Analog Circuit Design (Volumes 1, 2, & 3)** — *Jim Williams, Bob Dobkin*
* **Digital Electronics** — *Thomas L. Floyd*
* **Electronic Devices and Circuit Theory (11th Edition)** — *Robert L. Boylestad, Louis Nashelsky*

### 📁 [02_Signal_Integrity](./02_Signal_Integrity/)
Covers high-speed digital circuit design, signal integrity, and high-frequency board layouts:
* **High Speed Signal Propagation: Advanced Black Magic** — *Howard Johnson, Martin Graham*

### 📁 [03_Embedded_Systems](./03_Embedded_Systems/)
Includes documentation for microcontrollers, hardware platform specifications, and embedded architecture:
* **Embedded System Design**

### 📁 [04_Electrical_Engineering](./04_Electrical_Engineering/)
Comprehensive textbooks on general electrical engineering concepts and principles:
* **Electrical Engineering: Principles and Applications**
* **Principles and Applications of Electrical Engineering** — *Giorgio Rizzoni*
